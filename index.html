About Me

Hey there! I'm a sophomore at Syracuse University currently a dual majoring in Marketing and Advertising within the Whitman School of Management and the Newhouse School of Communications, respectively. Additionally, I'm on the Fashion & Beauty Communications track. Academically, I'm involved with the Delta Sigma Pi Professional Business Fraternity and the TNH Ad Agency.

Outside of academics, I'm passionate about many different creative outlets. Photography and videography are two hobbies that allow me to express myself visually, as well as turn into freelance work. Dance is also a way for me to wind down after a long school day. I love the feeling of dancing during football and basketball games, as well as prepping and working hard towards competition in the spring.
